
== Wii Mote Demo ==

1. Connect the bluetooth module to PORTJ
2. Change the MAC address in mac.c to the address printed on your wii mote.
3. make install
4. press the SYNC button on your wii mote

5. now the following should work:
  - the led on PORTA follows the accelerometer
  - button A enables the rumbler
  - button B stops the rumbler
